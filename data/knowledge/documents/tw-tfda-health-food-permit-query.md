# Health Food Permit Data Query

Source: https://consumer.fda.gov.tw/Food/InfoHealthFood.aspx?nodeID=162

## Extract

Official TFDA Consumer Knowledge Service Network page for health food permit lookup. Use it to confirm health food permit numbers, product names, registration status, approved health-care effect scope, and whether a product may lawfully present itself as health food in Taiwan. In review workflows, open this lookup before accepting label wording about health-food status, standard health-food logo use, permit number display, function claims, recommended intake, warning text, or supporting import documents. The lookup should be treated as an operational evidence source rather than a general guidance page: it links a label claim to a specific approved product record. If the product cannot be found or the permit scope does not match the label, route the case to health-food claim substantiation and false or exaggerated advertising review.
