# Health Food Permit Data Query

Source: https://consumer.fda.gov.tw/Food/InfoHealthFood.aspx?nodeID=162

## Extract

Official TFDA Consumer Knowledge Service Network page for health food permit lookup. Use it to confirm health food permit numbers, product names, applicant or permit holder context, registration status, approved health-care effect scope, standard health-food logo eligibility, and whether a product may lawfully present itself as health food in Taiwan. The live page is a database lookup and can be shallow or dynamic under automated collection, so LabelPass keeps this structured manual extract as the reusable search anchor while retaining the official URL for browser verification. Practical workflow: search by permit number, license number, product name, applicant name, brand name, functional ingredient, or Chinese health-food wording; capture the result record; compare approved effect scope with the label claim; verify permit number display, recommended intake, warning statement, target population, functional ingredient, dosage or serving instruction, expiration context, and whether supporting import documents match the product. Search aliases include health food permit, health food license, registration number, permit number, 保健食品, 健康食品, 健康食品許可證, 衛部健食字, 健康食品查詢, approved health-care effect, function claim, health food logo, 허가번호, 건강식품 허가, 보건효능, 기능성 문구, 대만 건강식품, and TFDA permit query. In review workflows, open this lookup before accepting claims about immunity, cholesterol, gastrointestinal function, blood sugar, liver protection, fatigue, bone health, body fat, dental health, or any approved health-care effect. If the product cannot be found, the permit number does not match, or the approved effect scope is narrower than the label wording, route the case to claim substantiation and false or exaggerated advertising review instead of ordinary food labeling approval.
