Interpretation letters DOH No. 0910065723, DOH No. 0910053196 and DOH No. 0910037302, issued by the former Department of Health of the Executive Yuan on October 15, 2002, September 3, 2002, and December 6, 2013, respectively, are no longer applicable. Article 2 Imported foods (including raw materials) that need to be repackaged, sub-packaged or further processed, the complete labeling in Chinese on the external packaging may not be required if there is identifiable original label or information for managing the products. For all other situations, Chinese labeling shall be completed prior to the sale (object including food factories, catering industry, or consumers) of the products.
