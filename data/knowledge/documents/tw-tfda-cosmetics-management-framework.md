The production source control includes ensuring that manufacturers comply with the Establishment Standards for Cosmetics Manufactory and promotion of Cosmetics Good Manufacturing Practice Regulations (GMP); the pre-marketing management includes notification of cosmetic products and establishment of product information file to replace the registration of specific purpose cosmetics; post-market surveillance focuses on the quality monitoring plan and inspections of the cosmetic products across counties and cities, the establishment of product adverse event reporting system for cosmetics, regular monitor of the safety alert for domestic and global cosmetics and strengthening consumer awareness of safe cosmetics use to create a comprehensive cosmetics quality and safety protection network.
