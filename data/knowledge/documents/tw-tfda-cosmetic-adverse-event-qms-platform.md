# TFDA Post-Market Quality Management System

Source: https://qms.fda.gov.tw/

## Extract

Official TFDA post-market quality management portal for cosmetics and related regulated products. Use this source when a Taiwan cosmetics review needs to confirm adverse-event reporting route, post-market surveillance workflow, defective product handling, or serious adverse effect escalation. The portal is operational and may require interactive browser access, account login, or form navigation, so the reusable knowledge record should be read together with the TFDA adverse-event reporting guidance and the MOJ serious adverse reporting regulation. For a product dossier, route checks include whether the responsible firm can identify the product, lot, importer or manufacturer, report date, event narrative, corrective action, recall linkage, and follow-up record. Label or claim screening should flag this source when product safety complaints, post-market incidents, recall triggers, or consumer injury wording appears in the file. Refresh by browser capture when the portal exposes downloadable forms or updated submission steps.
