Article 2 Both the maximum residue limits referred in the Standards and the actually analyzed residues of pesticides are calculated on the basis of the weights of the produces in the forms on market. The analysis of pesticide residues shall include the pesticide itself and its metabolites. Article 3 The pesticide residues in foods except those in animal products shall meet the Standards for the Pesticides Residue Limits in Foods Table and Extraneous Residue Limits Table, as Appendix Table 1 and Table 2. Pesticides not listed in the Table shall not be detected.
