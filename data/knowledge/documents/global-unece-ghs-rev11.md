Official UNECE index for the Globally Harmonized System of Classification and Labelling of Chemicals, eleventh revised edition, 2025. The source page routes to the free electronic edition and to language downloads including the English PDF file GHS Rev11e.pdf. Official document citation: ST/SG/AC.10/30/Rev.11. The GHS, also known as the Purple Book, is the global baseline used by many jurisdictions for chemical hazard classification and hazard communication. It supports regulatory checks for substance and mixture classification, physical hazards, health hazards, environmental hazards, signal words, hazard pictograms, hazard statements, precautionary statements, product identifiers, supplier identification, label elements, and safety data sheet terminology. Rev. 11 takes account of amendments adopted by the UN Committee of Experts on the Transport of Dangerous Goods and on the Globally Harmonized System of Classification and Labelling of Chemicals in December 2024.
