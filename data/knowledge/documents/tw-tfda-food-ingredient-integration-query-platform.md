# Food Ingredient Integration Query Platform

Source: https://www.fda.gov.tw/eng/newsContent.aspx?id=30794

## Extract

Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health Go To Content ::: Home Sitemap Contact us Bilingual Glossary 中文版 Go Hot： food additives, Nutrition label, Medicinal Products Food Drugs Controlled Drugs Medical Devices Cosmetics About Taiwan FDA ::: you are in： Home News &amp; Events News & Events Welcome to the "Food Ingredient Integration Query Platform" 【Update Date： 2024-10-31 】 unit：食品組 To make it easier for the public to assess the safety of ingredients used in food products, and to help food businesses take responsibility for self-regulation of food ingredients, the Taiwan Food and Drug Administration (TFDA) has established the "Food Ingredient Integration Query Platform." The "Food Ingredient Integration Query Platform" consolidates the results of ingredient safety evaluations over the past years, frequently asked questions from the public service mailbox, and related directive explanations. The platform provides information on food ingredients that have been evaluated as "suitable for food use" and those that are "not confirmed as safe for food use," offering a more comprehensive collection of food ingredient data. Currently, it encompasses 12 categories with a total of about 1,700 items, and it is continuously updated to provide the latest information. TFDA recommends reviewing the "Instructions and Notes" at the top of the webpage before searching to fully understand the platform’s purpose and how to use it. When making a search, you can enter Chinese, English, or scientific names as keywords to better confirm whether the platform contains the ingredient in question. When you obtain search results, please click on the hyperlink with the Chinese name of the ingredient to view detailed information about the item, such as recommended usage limits, permitted product types, or cautionary notes. It is essential to follow these limitations and label any relevant warnings accordingly. TFDA will continue to update the query platform’s data in the future. We encourage all stakeholders to make full use of the platform. For more information, please visit the "Food Ingredient Integration Query Platform" webpage (http://consumer.fda.gov.tw/Food/Material.aspx?nodeID=160), or access it via the FDA website (http://www.fda.gov.tw) by navigating to the main page / Business Areas / Food / Food Business Information Query / "Food Ingredient Integration Query Platform."
