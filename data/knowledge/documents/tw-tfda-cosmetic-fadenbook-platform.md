# TFDA Food and Drug Business Registration Platform

Source: http://fadenbook.fda.gov.tw/

## Extract

Official TFDA Food and Drug Business Registration Platform, commonly referenced as the fadenbook portal and 非登不可. Treat this as an operational companion source for Taiwan food, drug, and cosmetics business registration, operator account workflows, product notification, product registration, filing support, and responsible-firm routing. The portal is interactive and may not expose complete searchable text to automated HTTP clients, so LabelPass stores this manual operational extract as the stable knowledge anchor while the live official URL remains the verification authority. For cosmetics review, use it with TFDA cosmetic product registration platform evidence, product notification rules, PIF management evidence, GMP or manufacturer documents, and importer/responsible-firm data. Practical workflow: confirm whether the Taiwan responsible firm or importer has the right portal route, identify the product family, product name, cosmetic category, notification or registration obligation, PIF readiness, manufacturer or factory data, business registration context, account holder, submission status, and any mismatch between label owner, importer, and responsible firm.
